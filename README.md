# Privacy Policy for Grocify

**Last Updated: February 24, 2026**

## Introduction

Grocify ("we", "our", or "the app") is a grocery shopping and loyalty card management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Camera Access

- **What:** We access your device camera to scan barcodes and QR codes on loyalty cards.
- **Why:** To quickly add loyalty cards to your digital wallet.
- **Storage:** Scanned barcode data is stored locally on your device only.

### Location Data

- **What:** We access your device location when you search for nearby stores.
- **Why:** To show you relevant store locations and distances.
- **Storage:** Location data is used in real-time only and is NOT stored or retained.

### Loyalty Card Information

- **What:** Card names, barcode numbers, and card images you manually enter or scan.
- **Why:** To display your loyalty cards for use at checkout.
- **Storage:** All loyalty card data is stored locally on your device only.

### Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to collect anonymous usage statistics, including the number of active users, session counts, and general device information (such as device model, operating system version, and country). This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

No advertising identifiers are collected. This information is used solely to improve the app experience and understand usage patterns.

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage Only:** All your personal data (loyalty cards, preferences) is stored exclusively on your device.
- **No Cloud Upload:** We do NOT upload your personal data to any servers.
- **No Account Required:** Grocify does not require user accounts or registration.
- **Encryption:** Sensitive data is encrypted using your device's secure storage.

## Third-Party Services

The app connects to external APIs to:

- Display product information
- Show store locations
- Retrieve current specials and prices

These requests include your general location (when searching for stores) but no personally identifiable information.

The app uses Firebase Analytics (a Google service) to collect anonymous, aggregated usage data. For more information on how Google processes this data, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:

- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your personal information with advertisers
- Track your behavior for marketing purposes

Anonymous, aggregated usage statistics are collected via Firebase Analytics to help us improve the app. This data cannot identify individual users.

## Your Rights

You can:

- **Delete your data** at any time by deleting individual loyalty cards or uninstalling the app
- **Deny permissions** for camera or location (some features will be limited)
- **Use the app offline** for viewing saved loyalty cards

## Children's Privacy

Grocify is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
